Jackson Wang

His music


Jackson Wang is a talented singer, dancer, and rapper who rose to fame as a member of the South Korean boy band Got7. In addition to his successful career with the group, Jackson has also established himself as a solo artist.


He began releasing music as a solo artist in 2018 with the single "Papillon" and has since released multiple singles and an EP titled "Mirrors". His solo music often showcases his unique style, blending elements of hip-hop, R&B, and pop to create a sound that is both energetic and versatile.


He has also performed in a variety of shows, music festivals and tours around the world.

Latest Album Release

GOT7’s Jackson Scores His Highest Entry On The Billboard 200 With “MAGIC MAN”

On September 19 local time, Billboard announced the debuts on their latest Billboard 200 chart, which ranks the most popular albums in the United States. Jackson’s latest album “MAGIC MAN” comes in at No. 15, marking the artist’s highest-charting album! In 2019, Jackson made his debut on the Billboard 200 at No. 32 with his first full solo album “Mirrors.”
